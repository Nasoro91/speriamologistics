ABOUT US

Speriamo company limited is a privately held clearance company registered in Tanzania. The company is based in Dar es salaam, it offers services in clearing and forwarding.

Mission

To provide seamless, efficient, and cost-effective logistics solutions by ensuring the timely and secure movement of goods across borders while upholding integrity, transparency, and compliance with international trade regulations.

Vision

Our vision at Speriamo Company Limited is to be the leading clearing and forwarding company in Tanzania.

Core Values

These are principles that guide our operations and customer interactions. These values help us establish trust, efficiency, and reliability in the logistics and shipping industry. Our core values include:

Integrity and Transparency:
Upholding honesty and fairness in all dealings with clients, customs authorities, and partners.

Efficiency and Timeliness:
Ensuring goods are cleared and forwarded on time to minimize delays.

Professionalism:
Maintaining high standards of service, including accuracy in documentation and compliance with international trade regulations.

Compliance and Accountability:
Adhering to all legal and regulatory requirements, such as customs laws and international trade standards.
